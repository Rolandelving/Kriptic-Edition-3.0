About Kriptic Edition 3.0

Kriptic Edition is a comprehensive entertainment platform developed over 600+ days with dedication to quality and user experience. This is the third major iteration, representing a complete overhaul from the previous Google Sites version.

The platform combines a Netflix-style movie interface, instant-launch game technology, community polls, and various utility tools into a unified astronomy-themed experience.

Development Journey

Version 1.0: Initial concept and basic website structure
Version 2.0: Google Sites implementation with expanded content
Version 3.0: Independent hosting with advanced features and professional design

All design decisions, content curation, and project management handled personally, with AI assistance for coding implementation.
